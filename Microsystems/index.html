What is the problem Cameras and sensors are used as key components of an automobile
due to the expansion of autonomous driving. However, the various contaminants (rain, fog, snow, oil, dust, etc.)
generated on the surfaces cause errors in the cameras and sensors, threatening the safety of the drivers.
How to solve In order to resolve such problems, MICROSYSTEMS develops the world's first electronic self-cleaning Drop Free Glass (DFG)
that can promptly remove various contaminants on the surfaces of the cameras and sensors using a unique microfluidic technology. more +
Why is Drop Free Glass Drop Free Glass (DFG) uses electric fields unlike the existing mechanical technologies; hence,
there is no need for a separate external driving device,
and various contaminants can be immediately removed with low power consumption. more +
What we provide Since Drop Free Glass (DFG) is easy to miniaturize under the simple structure and has low power consumption,
it can be applied to various products. Here are some products with Drop Free Glass. more +
